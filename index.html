A Fairytale Vacation

Cappadocia is a touristic location where exceptional natural wonders exsist. There are fairy chimneys and a unique historical and cultural heritage. Hot-air ballooning is very popular in Cappadocia and is available in Goreme. Trekking is enjoyed in Ihlara Valley. You can even stay in cave hotels and wake up to beautiful views of hot-air ballooons floating in the sky.
